# Privacy Policy — SetAsideTracker

**Last updated: June 27, 2026**

This policy describes how SetAsideTracker ("the app," "we," "us") handles information. It's written to accurately describe what the app actually does today, not generic boilerplate — read it alongside the app's actual behavior described below.

## The short version

SetAsideTracker stores everything you enter **only on your own device**. We don't have a server, we don't have user accounts, and as of this writing nothing you enter is transmitted anywhere. If that ever changes (see "Future changes" below), we'll update this policy and the in-app disclosure before it does.

## What information the app collects

- **Profile info** (name, email): entered during onboarding, used only to personalize the app's greeting. Never transmitted — stored locally on your device only.
- **Tax profile** (filing status, dependents, state/county, income details you choose to enter): used entirely on-device to calculate your estimated tax set-aside. Never transmitted.
- **Earnings entries** (platform, date, pay, tips, mileage, expenses, hours worked): the core data you log. Stored locally on-device only.
- **App settings** (app lock on/off, light/dark mode preference): stored locally on-device only.

None of the above is sent to us, to any server, or to any third party. There is no backend — the app works the same whether or not your device has an internet connection.

## How your data is protected

- All of the data above is **encrypted at rest** on your device using a randomly generated key stored in your device's secure hardware-backed storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore).
- Optional **app lock** uses your device's own Face ID, Touch ID, fingerprint, or passcode. The app never receives or stores your biometric data itself — your device's operating system handles authentication and only tells the app "yes" or "no."

## Data you explicitly choose to export

The app has an optional "Export Data (CSV)" and "Create Backup File" feature in Settings. These let *you* generate a file containing your data and save or share it wherever *you* choose (e.g. your device's Files app, a cloud drive, AirDrop, email). We have no visibility into or control over what happens to that file once you've exported it — that's between you and wherever you choose to send it.

## Crash reporting and analytics

The app has crash-reporting and basic analytics instrumentation built in, but **neither is currently active** — no crash reports or analytics events are being sent anywhere as of this writing. If we enable either in the future, we will update this policy first to describe exactly what's collected (e.g. anonymized crash logs to help us fix bugs) and update the app's data-collection disclosures with Apple/Google accordingly.

## Third parties

We don't share, sell, or transmit your data to any third party, because we don't transmit your data anywhere at all. The app doesn't include third-party advertising SDKs.

## Children's privacy

SetAsideTracker is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Your choices

- You can edit or delete any entry, your profile, or your tax profile at any time within the app.
- "Clear All Data" in Settings permanently deletes everything stored on your device.
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data (subject to your device's own backup behavior — see below).

## Device backups

Because your data lives in your device's standard app-storage area, it may be included in your device's own backup system (e.g. iCloud Backup, Android's auto-backup) the same way any other app's data would be, depending on your device settings. We have no visibility into or control over those backups — that's between you and Apple/Google.

## Changes to this policy

If how we handle data changes — for example, if we add a real account system, cloud sync, or activate analytics/crash reporting — we'll update this policy and notify you within the app before the change takes effect.
